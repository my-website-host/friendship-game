How to Play & Controls 🎮

🏃‍♂️ Movement & Jumps

Press SPACE, Arrow Up (↑), or click/tap the screen to jump. Tap again mid-air to Double Jump!

✴️ Weapon Support

Press SHIFT (or F, X, Enter) or tap the red THROW button on screen to throw a Ninja Star. Use it to shatter standard hazard blocks!

💖 Health & Lives

You have 3 lives. Hitting a regular hazard block costs 1 life and gives you 1.5 seconds of flashing invulnerability. Don't run out of lives!

🎁 Collectibles & Power-ups

❤️ Heart: +1 Life (max 3)
✴️ Star: +1 Ammo (max 4)
🧲 Magnet: Automatically attracts the next 2 memory treasures!

🏆 Main Objective

Guide the chosen runner through a timeline of shared memories. You MUST collect every single memory treasure along the run to unlock and open the final birthday surprise gift at the checkered finish line! If you miss even one, the vault stays locked!
